About Crazy Joe's Burger House!

Crazy Joe's Burger House was started in 1957 by Joseph Jackson. Knowing the average household was beginning to become more and more frantic in their daily activities, Jackson wanted to provide families with a delicious, fast option for lunch and dinner.

For over 50 years, since it's first restaurant was opened in Quincy, Illnois, Crazy Joe's Burger House has served over a billion customers! Nationwide there are 1,500 locations in cities large and small.
