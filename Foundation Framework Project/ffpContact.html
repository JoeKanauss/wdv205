Contact Us

Here at Maureen's Children, we know that being a witch in today's age is hard. We may have gained a few legal rights, but every day is still an uphill battle. We must contend with hatred from those outside of our community, and we must suffer through legal injustices.

Maureen's Children invites you to contact us. Speak with us about troubles in your life, sign up for offered classes, ask questions, or seek out a personal counselor from your local center. Whatever the reason, a representative of Maureen's Children is always on hand to quickly and kindly respond to your messages.
