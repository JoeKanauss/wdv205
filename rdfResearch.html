Responsive Design Framework

Identify the framework your researched.

The framework I chose to demonstrate is Foundation.

Who wrote or supports the framework?

Foundation was created by a company called ZURB

List some companies or organizations using this framework.

Companies that use the Foundation framework include Ebay, HP, Disney, and Ford.

Briefly describe the framework and its primary features.

Foundation is a mobile-first, customizable framework, and its primary features include grid layouts, navigation layouts, containers, and media layouts.

What are some of the advantages/disadvantages to using this framework?

A huge advantage to Foundation is its customizability, allowing one to use it, but not be limited. A disadvantage to Foundation is the fact that it is constantly changing. What could be a feature of the framework one day may not be the next.
